Frequently Asked Questions

+

What is the cost for your services?

To get an accurate estimate for our services, please contact us directly. We’ll discuss your project requirements, including site functionality, design needs, and other specifics. This will allow us to provide a preliminary quote. If you wish to proceed, we’ll arrange a visit to your business to finalize a detailed quote.

+

How long will the design process take?

Project timelines are generally influenced by client deadlines. If you have a specific timeline in mind, we will do our best to accommodate it. The primary factor affecting the timeline is the receipt of content and feedback from the client.

+

How can I start working with Nexus Inc.?

To get started, reach out to us with your project details. We’ll review your requirements and discuss potential engagement models. Once we agree on the best approach, we’ll begin the development process.

+

Can you enhance the professionalism of my existing site?

Absolutely. Provide us with your current site details and requirements, and our experienced team will revamp your site to give it a more professional and appealing look.

+

What are the payment terms?

We typically structure payments in three installments: at the start, midway, and upon project completion. However, we are flexible and can adjust the payment schedule to suit your needs. We accept checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards to facilitate the process.
